The Path to Mars Independence

2025 - 2125

A century of colonization, oppression, and liberation

Foundation & Early Years

2 / 10
  • 2025: Humanity arrives at Mars and establishes Olympus Colony with 20 inhabitants as a collective effort by global space agencies (SUA).
  • 2030: First Martian-born human, Astreus Astley, son of astronaut Paul Astley (UK) and biologist Agatha Santos (Brazil).
  • 2037: Tourism begins ($50K round trip); Olympus Sky Palace hotel opens with 100 luxury rooms.
  • 2042: Population reaches 1,000 inhabitants.
  • 2047-2053: "Climate Wars" erupt on Earth due to resource scarcity.
  • 2048: Olympus reaches 10,000 inhabitants and becomes a city under the Space Union Agency (SUA).

Growth & Earth Crisis

3 / 10
  • 2053: World Government created; nation-states retain autonomy; Project Gaia (terraforming research) begins under SUA.
  • 2060: Olympus reaches 100K inhabitants; Mars offers better opportunities than poverty-stricken Earth regions.
  • 2061: Astreus returns to Mars; Paul Astley retires; Amanda Maikov becomes scientific leader.
  • 2065: First generation shows physical/cultural differences; racial tensions emerge (20% Martian-born, 72% adult immigrants).
  • 2069: Olympus reaches 200K; Astreus becomes leader defending Martian rights against "martianophobes."
  • 2070: Astreus declares: "Our planet is Mars, our city is Olympus."

The Terraquean Occupation

4 / 10
  • 2077: Earth Government dissolves nation-states; Mars loses autonomous "nation-state" status.
  • 2079: Earth military occupies Olympus; protests erupt.
  • 2081: Marcian Action (MA) party begins armed resistance.
  • 2082: Amanda Maikov arrested for supporting MA, dies in custody (alleged suicide) at age 66.
  • 2083: Astreus (53) publicly confesses involvement, sentenced to 15 years on Earth. His famous words: "My crime was loving Mars and its people too much."
  • 2084: SUA dissolved, merged into Earth Space Agency (ESA); resistance crushed.

The Peaceful Resistance

5 / 10
  • 2088: Stela Astley (Astreus's daughter) founds "Marcian Freedom" (MF) party on Earth.
  • 2090: MF wins 3% of Earth Congress, 9% on Mars.
  • 2093: Mass protests demand autonomy and Astreus's release; Selena (first "Selenita") joins the cause.
  • 2094: Astreus released at age 63 after 11 years imprisonment.
  • 2095: Astreus elected President of Mars in first round at age 65; Earth military withdraws.

Independence Achieved

6 / 10
  • 2096: Mars regains nation-state status; terraforming deadline extended to 2112.
  • 2099: Full independence ratified; Marcian Space Agency (MSA) created.
  • 2100: Mars population: 1.1M (Olympus 900K, Cydonia 200K). Trade balance fixed at max equality (39/61).
  • 2103: Vladimir Maikov (Amanda's son) elected Mars President; honors his mother, promises justice for "Terraquean Occupation" victims.
  • Lunar colony "Luara" reaches 200K; Selena (32) founds "Selenitian Democracy" (SD) party.

Terraforming & Expansion

7 / 10
  • 2106: Mars terraforming completion announced for 2112; trials for "Terraquean Occupation" crimes begin.
  • 2107: Selena (36) elected Lunar President; SD wins.
  • 2110: Mars fully terraformed! Domes opened; atmosphere stabilized; moss and plants spread across planet.
  • 2111: Astreus Astley dies of natural causes at 81; his daughter Stela present.
  • 2112: 30-meter statue of Astreus unveiled at Martian Freedom Square with bronze plaques honoring occupation victims.

The Earth Empire Era

8 / 10
  • 2113: Mars achieves independent military, ends relations with Earth Empire.
  • 2115: Selena (44) leaves Lunar presidency; remains SD party leader.
  • 2120: "Earth Empire" formally established; Luna and Venus included as special "planet-states."
  • Luna: Luara 800K, Luana 50K; Mars: Olympus 1.1M, Cydonia 400K; Venus: Venera 10K.

Legacy & Future

9 / 10
  • 2125: 100th anniversary of Mars landing celebrated.
  • Population Milestones:
    • Olympus: 5M inhabitants
    • Cydonia: 1.5M
    • Luna: 1.1M
    • Venus (Venera): 200K
  • 2125: Stela Astley (68) retires from politics, returns to Mars as professor at Olimpus University.
  • 2125: Selena (54) begins second term as Lunar President.
